ABOUT

About

I've always liked the moment when data stops being a spreadsheet and starts changing what someone decides to do next. That pull took me from a statistics degree at Nottingham to a Master's in Data Science in Health at UCLA — and, along the way, from just analyzing data to building the products around it.

My internships let me do both. At an AI mock-interview startup I designed the A/B tests and funnel analysis that lifted user engagement by 85%, and built a scoring model that made marketing spend 30% more efficient. Elsewhere I've built ETL pipelines across 50+ on-chain protocols and turned 100k+ records into dashboards teams actually used. But the part I kept gravitating toward was shipping the whole thing — so I started building my own: full-stack web apps with Next.js, React and Supabase, an AI photo-editing tool on FastAPI, and a couple of e-commerce brands I run end to end.

What changed how I work is AI tooling. With agentic coding — Claude Code, MCP, the whole new stack — I can go from a rough idea to a deployed product in days, not months. I think that's where data people are heading: not just modeling or just engineering, but forward-deployed — sitting close to a real problem and building the model and the tool that solves it.

Off-screen I cycle, hike, and play badminton — the same appetite for reps and small improvements I bring to everything else. Always happy to trade ideas with people who care about data, AI tooling, and the "why" behind what we build.
